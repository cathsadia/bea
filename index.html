Cover Letter

A cover letter is a short, professional letter you send with your resume when applying for a job.

Application letter of Intent

A letter of intent is a general overview of your industry-specific skills, experienced and the reason you're interested in working for a specific employer.

Resume

A resume is a concise document that outlines an individual's work experience, education and skills to showcase their qualifications for a job.

Different types of Resume

  • Chronological resume
    List your work experiences and achievememnts starting from the current or most recent one, and following up with previous jobs below.
  • Functional Resume
    A Functional resume, also known as a skills-based resume, is a resume style that focuses on displaying your skills, rather than previous work history.
  • Combination resume
    A Combination resume is a versatile format that merges elements of both chronological and functional resumes.
